Hack Club of Rancho Cotate High School

Hello! Rancho Tech Makers is a club that supports the interests of future computer science students at Rancho Cotate High School. We are teen hackers that meet two days a week at room M6 on Thursdays and Fridays. Together, we work on hands-on projects where learn coding and hardware skills that transfer to our future careers!

Rancho Tech Makers

Hello! Rancho Tech Makers is a chapter of Hack Club that supports the interests of future computer science students at Rancho Cotate High School. We are teen hackers that meet two days a week at room M6 on Thursdays and Fridays. Together, we work on hands-on projects where we learn coding and hardware skills that transfer to our future careers!

Why Join Us?

You don't need prior experience to join. We are a welcoming, diverse group of students who share similar interests. Nobody feels left out after they join!

We work hands on and directly with projects; as much of our time as possible is spent building either building electrical/hardware projects or programming projects! There is plenty to do!

At heart, we are a passionate group that is under the Hack Club 501(c)3 organization, and projects are rewarded with free food (like boba) or money to fund projects!
